About our page

The idea for our page came from a recent reading project. We had so many books to choose from. Reading books take away valuable time that we can't waste, and because of that it was difficult for us to choose a book that's worth our time. That's how this website is born.

Our values

Our number one value is integrity. We aim to provide you the most unbiased, neutral reviews possible. We are a non-profit project. Our ultimate goal here is for you to find books that will sure be worth your time, as well as to help you find yourself friendly communities that might share the same passion into books with you.
